Aloo Matar Ki Sabji

Indian Vegetarian

Vegetarian Spicy North Indian

Aloo Matar Ki Sabji

Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add all dry spices and mix well.
  6. Add cubed potatoes and peas, mix to coat with masala.
  7. Add 1 cup water, cover and cook for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with roti.

Chicken Biryani

Indian Non-Vegetarian

Non-Veg Spicy Rice Dish

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Saffron strands
  • Mint and coriander leaves
  • Fried onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Soak rice for 30 minutes, then boil until 70% cooked.
  3. In a heavy-bottomed pan, layer marinated chicken.
  4. Add a layer of partially cooked rice over the chicken.
  5. Sprinkle saffron milk, fried onions, mint, and coriander.
  6. Seal the pan with dough or aluminum foil.
  7. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes (dum cooking).
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Margherita Pizza

Italian

Vegetarian Italian Baked

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Oregano for seasoning

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, knead into smooth dough.
  3. Cover and let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled.
  4. Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F).
  5. Roll dough into pizza base, spread pizza sauce.
  6. Add sliced mozzarella and tomato slices.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil and oregano before serving.

Paneer Butter Masala

Indian Vegetarian

Vegetarian Creamy Restaurant Style

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 10-12 cashews, soaked
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat butter and oil, sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Grind soaked cashews into a smooth paste.
  5. Add cashew paste to the gravy, cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add all spices, salt, and sugar.
  7. Add paneer cubes and cream, simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Crush kasuri methi and sprinkle on top before serving.

Vanilla Ice Cream

Dessert

Dessert No-cook Chilled

Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 4 egg yolks (optional for custard base)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix milk, sugar, and salt until sugar dissolves.
  2. Add heavy cream and vanilla extract, whisk well.
  3. For richer ice cream: whisk egg yolks with sugar until pale.
  4. Heat milk until steaming, slowly pour over yolks while whisking.
  5. Return mixture to heat, cook until it coats the back of a spoon.
  6. Chill mixture completely (at least 4 hours or overnight).
  7. Pour into ice cream maker and churn according to instructions.
  8. Transfer to container, freeze for 4-6 hours until firm.

Vegetable Hakka Noodles

Chinese

Vegetarian Quick Stir-fry

Vegetable Hakka Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g hakka noodles
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 capsicum, sliced
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles according to package instructions, drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok, add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Add carrots and stir-fry for 2 minutes on high heat.
  4. Add capsicum and cabbage, stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce, sugar, salt and pepper.
  6. Add boiled noodles and toss everything together.
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes until everything is well combined.
  8. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Italian

Vegetarian Creamy Comfort Food

Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg powder
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Additional Parmesan for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Add grated Parmesan cheese, stir until melted and smooth.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg powder.
  7. Add cooked pasta to the sauce, toss to coat evenly.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan before serving.

Vanilla Sponge Cake

Dessert

Baking Dessert Celebration

Vanilla Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Mix in vanilla extract.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Alternately add flour mixture and milk to butter mixture, beginning and ending with flour.
  7. Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth the top.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  9. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
