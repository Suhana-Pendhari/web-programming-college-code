Ultimate Chocolate Cake Recipe

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making a rich, moist chocolate cake from scratch. This recipe serves 8-10 people and takes about 1 hour to prepare and bake. Let's get baking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the boiling water (the batter will be thin). Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on wire racks.
  7. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting and enjoy!

Tips

  • For extra moisture, add a cup of chocolate chips to the batter.
  • Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.
  • This cake pairs perfectly with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
